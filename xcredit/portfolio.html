Labelz & Love

Last semester, I took IST 486 and learned all about social media and the different algorithms it encompasses. I found that this was one of my most interesting classes because it related to my everyday life. Prior to this class, I rarely gave each post, caption, location, and tag extreme thought. However, after taking this class I have a whole different outlook when browsing my feed. One of the projects in the class allowed me to learn and explore these algorithms hands on. My group and I focused our studies on fashion. We researched the best way to reach our audience and what posts would look aesthetically pleasing on our feed. Writing 105

During my freshman year at Syracuse, I took the course Writing 105. I thoroughly enjoyed this course as I felt that it enhanced my analyzation skills while also allowing me to learn more about myself as a student and a person. One of the assignments in class was to create a theme based collage with photos you took that represent a part of who you are. My theme was New York City. Each photo I chose I related to a song that reminded me of where I lived, along with pictures which held significant meaning to me.
